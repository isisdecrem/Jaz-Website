Our 3 step design process

Talk

The first step to getting your custom shoes is to have a chat with Jaz about the design you'd like. After this talk, Jaz will be able to set a price and tell you how long the design process will take. She will be collecting your shoes after this.

Sketch

Next, Jaz will send you pictures of a sketch of your shoes with color. This will help to make sure you see eye to eye. If you give the sketch the okay, Jaz will start desigining. If not, she will change the sketch based on your requests.

Paint

Jaz will begin painting your shoes. She uses no harmful chemicals, and gives your shoes a little wash beforehand. When finished, she will spay your shoes so the paint lasts. You will pay for your shoes when she gives them to you as quality assurance.

About Us

Who

Jaz Decrem is the founder of The Shoo Shak as well as the designer. Jaz is a 13 year old girl based in Palo Alto. Jaz has been an artist her whole life and started The Shoo Shak to combine her love of art and streetwear.

Where

We are located in Midtown Palo Alto. As of right now most of our clients are locally based so we will meet the clients face to face. We do accept clients from different areas as long as they pay for the shipping of their shoes.

Awards

Within the first week of launching The Shoo Shak, Jaz was overwhelmed with clients. Following the creation of the company, Jaz entered a Nike competition where she photoshop designed Air Max shoes. Jaz got second place and recieved a $115 Nike gift card.
